Instructions

Challange yourself against computer in famous Tic Tac Toe game!

Player marker: green.
Computer marker: red.

Click START to start the game.
Click RESET to reset the game.
Click to mute the sounds.
Click to unmute the sounds.

You have 15 seconds to make a move, after that the move is passed to computer. Markers 'X' and 'O' are selected randomly for player and computer. Click the field to make a move. To win a game you must place 3 markers next to each other horizontally, vertically or diagonally on the gameboard. You can mute the sounds by clicking volume icon. Below START and RESET buttons there is counter for your and computer wins.
